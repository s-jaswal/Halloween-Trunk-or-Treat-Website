Halloween Trunk or Treat Schedule

Event Start

The event kicks off with a parade of spooky trunks and costumes. Be there by 5:00 PM to catch the start!

Costume Contest

Join the Halloween costume contest at 6:00 PM. Prizes will be awarded for scariest, funniest, and most creative costumes.

Trunk Judging

The trunk judging begins at 7:00 PM. Show off your spooky decorations and win the title of Best Trunk!

Candy Distribution

At 8:00 PM, it's time to trick or treat! Grab your bag and enjoy a sweet candy-filled adventure!
